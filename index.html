Manufacturing Excellence

Carpet Tiles

Welspun offers premium, durable, and easy-to-install carpet tiles featuring advanced stain resistance and sound insulation. They come in versatile, eco-friendly designs, making them ideal for high-traffic commercial and modern office spaces.

Home Furnishing Textiles

Welspun Living is a global leader in home textiles, specializing in bath linens (towels, robes, mats) and bedding solutions (sheets, comforters, pillows). They are highly recognized for patented innovations like HygroCotton® (which gets softer with every wash) and supplying major retail chains worldwide.

TMT Rebars

Welspun Shield TMT rebars offer high strength, superior earthquake resistance, and excellent flexibility due to advanced thermo-mechanical treatment. They also feature outstanding corrosion resistance and weldability, making them ideal for heavy-duty infrastructure and high-rise construction.

Tubes And Bars

Welspun manufactures a premium range of integrated stainless steel and alloy steel seamless tubes, pipes, and rolled/bright bars. Engineered for high-precision and extreme environments, they offer excellent corrosion resistance and durability for critical industries like oil and gas, aerospace, and power.

Fire Safety Fabrics

Welspun manufactures advanced, inherently flame-retardant fire safety fabrics designed for protective gear, thermal liners, and fire blankets. Engineered for extreme heat resistance and durability, they provide crucial thermal defense and moisture barriers for high-risk environments
